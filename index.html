Our Story

A School Built on
Purpose & Care

Radiant Public School, situated at the Last Bus Stop on Panjabari Road in Batahguli, Guwahati, is a recognized institution committed to providing students with a balanced, nurturing, and forward-thinking education.

We believe every child holds extraordinary potential. Our school environment fosters curiosity, creative thinking, and ethical character — preparing students not just for exams, but for life.

🌟 Mission

To nurture every child's potential through balanced academics, creativity, and values.

🔭 Vision

To be Guwahati's most trusted school, shaping confident, compassionate leaders.

💛 Values

Integrity, excellence, inclusivity, and a lifelong love of learning.

Required Documents
